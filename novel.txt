He didn't know why they were there... but they were.
Suddenly they started running at him with spears.
Predictably, as anyone is likely to do when a random mob runs at you with spears, and probably malicious intent... he ran.
His heart froze as he heard a member of the mob shout…
The world was a blur, the sun an icicle.
“EVERYTHING IS PURPLE!”
Said the man to the mob.
